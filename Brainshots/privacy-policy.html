Brain Shots Privacy Policy

Last Modified: August 1, 2017

Brain Shots Inc. is a software company focused on creating game apps that fuse social interaction, humour and friendly competition. "Brain Shots", "we" or "us" refer to Brain Shots Inc. This Privacy Policy applies whenever you play our games, use our websites, mobile applications or other online products and services (collectively, the "Services").

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so we encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the Services to stay informed about our information collection and handling practices. If you object to any changes we make, you should close your account, otherwise you consent to these changes by continuing to use the services. If you have any concerns about the information provided by you or collected by our service in accordance with the Brain Shots Privacy Policy and Terms of Use, you should not use our Services.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect information you provide directly to us. To create or configure a profile, you are required to provide certain personal information, including your name, country of residence, and gender. When you play the game, the app will also display the name you have given to your phone to the other players. You may also use a nickname. You may provide this information by filling in forms or by logging in to your Facebook account. You understand that by entering this information, we and others will be able to identify you by your profile. You also understand that your information may be listed publicly on the Services and your device name, or nickname if you have one, will be displayed to the other players, If you play the game through your profile, we may also keep a record of your gameplay, for example, how many games you have played, and whether or not you won a game. If you do not wish to provide this personal information, you may access the Services as a guest.

If you purchase add-ons, you will be making a payment through a third party (like Apple), and you are agreeing to that third party's payment terms and personal information handling practices and you understand that Brain Shots is not a party to the transaction.

When you ask for help from our customer support team, we will collect and store the information you provide (generally your name, phone number and email address). We will store the communications you have with the customer support team and any information in those communications.

We may communicate with you through email, Facebook and other means based on your messaging preferences which can be modified by you at any time. Communications may relate to promotions, service availability or other service-related issues. You are not able to opt-out of certain communications, including those relating to functions or features of the services, or technical or security notices. For information on unsubscribing from our marketing or promotional emails, please see below.

You may be able to invite your friends to play with you, in which case, you agree that "friends" are those with whom you have a personal or professional relationship. Prior to inviting your friends to play with you, you must have permission to use your friend's email address for the purpose of inviting your friend to play the game. You must not send: (i) multiple invitations to the same friend; (ii) unsolicited emails; or (iii) spam. If you choose to send such invitations, you represent to Brain Shots that the recipients of such invitations have consented to receive such messages from you.

We may use the information we collect to:

  • operate and improve our Services and our players' experiences;
  • allow players to identify you through the Services and invite you to play with them
  • allow you to invite friends and family to play the game and use the Services;
  • allow players to communicate with each other through the Services;
  • offer players in-game coupons or other incentives from third parties
  • create reports or analysis of the Services for the purpose of business intelligence, marketing or research;
  • comply with our legal obligations or resolve any disputes we may have with you or other players;
  • protect Brain Shots rights and property in connection with the Services;
  • notify players of in-game updates;
  • prevent cheating, fraud or potentially illegal activities and to enforce or Terms of Use; and
  • provide technical support.

We will ask for your consent before using information for a purpose other than those that are set out in this Privacy Policy.

We don't sell any of your personal information to third parties for direct marketing.

We may employ third party companies and individuals to facilitate our Services. They have limited access to your information only to perform certain tasks on our behalf and are obligated to Brain Shots not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

We may remove parts of data that can identify you and share anonymized data with other parties. We may also combine your information with other information in a way that it is no longer associated with you and share that aggregated information.

If Brain Shots becomes involved with a merger, acquisition or asset sale, we will continue to protect the confidentiality of personal information and will use and disclose in accordance with the law.

Parties with whom you may share your information

Any information or content you put on your profile may be seen by others and may be available to the public. By providing personal information to us you are expressly accepting the terms and conditions of our Terms of Use and accepting and agreeing to our processing of your personal information in ways set out by this Privacy Policy. Should you no longer consent to our collecting and processing of this information you can change your account settings or your profile, or close your account, in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.

You may allow the Services to interact with Facebook, a third party social media platform which will provide information about you to Brain Shots. Since any other application or platform is owned by a third party, you must ensure that you read their Terms of Use and privacy policy.

Responding to legal requests

Brain Shots may need to disclose personal information, profile information, or information about your activities when required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we have a good faith belief that disclosure is reasonably necessary and permitted by law.

We may process your information outside the country where you live.

By using the Services, you consent to the transfer of information outside the country you live in. You understand that if your information is processed in a foreign country, it may be accessible to law enforcement and national security authorities within that jurisdiction.

You can change your Brain Shots profile information at any time by editing your profile or by uninstalling the app. If you close your account your personal information will generally be removed from the Services within 24 hours. We retain the personal information you provide while your account is in existence or as needed to provide you service.
